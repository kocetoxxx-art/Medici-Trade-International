INSTITUTIONAL TRADE SECURITY & SOP

Strict compliance under ICC regulations. Capital protection guaranteed via tier-one banking instruments.

DLC (Documentary Letter of Credit)

Irrevocable, Transferable, Confirmed L/C at sight issued via top world banks, payable strictly post-SGS validation at origin loading port.

SBLC (Standby Letter of Credit)

Secure financial structuring designed for long-term annual contract allocations, protecting revolving credit line volumes.

T/T (Telegraphic Transfer)

Flexible commercial payment options scaled strictly against verified export logs, phytosanitary logs, and loading port documentation.

Corporate Profile

Medici Trade International is a premier global commodity trading group and strategic supply partner, specializing in the two-way import, export, and distribution of high-value B2B foodstuffs and commercial allocations. Headquartered in Europe (Plovdiv, Bulgaria), our operational reach spans a powerful multi-continental network, executing seamless trade corridors across the European Union, Mainland China, Hong Kong, the UAE, the Middle East, the United States, South America, South Africa, and Thailand.

From direct-from-origin sourcing, strict plant vetting, and international compliance (GACC, CITES, Halal) to complex global freight execution, we handle the heavy operations from end to end. At Medici Trade International, we ensure your capital connects directly to verified product volume under bulletproof transaction security.
